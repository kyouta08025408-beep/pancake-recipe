        <h2>Ingredients</h2>
        <ul>
          <li>3 eggs</li>
          <li>100ml of milk</li>
          <li>200g of mashed sweet potatos</li>
          <li>10g of no salt butter</li>
          <li>15g of honey</li>
          <li>40g of protein(any flavor is ok)</li>
          <li>5g of baking powder</li>
          <li>5g of MCT oil</li>
          <li>80g of rice powder</li>
          <li>7g of creatine</li>
        </ul>
        <h2>Instructions</h2>
        <ol>
          <li>Wash sweet potatos and cut in round form.</li>
          <li>Steam cutted potatos for 5 minutes with 600W in microwae.</li>
          <li>Mash potatos with butter</li>
          <li>Take 3 eggs and milk and MCT oil into potato bowl and mix well.</li>
          <li>Take the rest powders into the bowl and mix togather.</li>
          <li>Pour material into heat resistant box and bake in microwave at 600W appropriately</li>
